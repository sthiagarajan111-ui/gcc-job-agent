One-time Setup Required

This dashboard needs a free RapidAPI key to pull live Bayut/DLD property data. Register takes 5 minutes, no credit card needed.

Setup steps

1
Go to rapidapi.com → Sign Up free (no credit card)
2
Search "Bayut" → select "Bayut API" by bayutapi.com → click Subscribe to Test (free — 750 calls/month)
3
Copy your X-RapidAPI-Key from the dashboard
4
Go to Render dashboard → gcc-job-agent → Environment → Add:
RAPIDAPI_KEY = your-key-here → Save Changes
5
Render will auto-redeploy. Come back here and click ⟳ Refresh in the top right. Data loads in ~60 seconds and caches for 12 hours.
